# Palm Island — Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** 2026-04-24
**Last updated:** 2026-04-24

Palm Island is developed and published by Portal Dragon LLC ("we", "us", "our"). This policy explains what data the Palm Island mobile app collects, how we use it, and the choices you have.

If you have questions about anything below, email **info@portaldragon.com**.

---

## 1. Data we collect

When you play Palm Island, the app collects the following:

### Gameplay analytics (automatic)
- Game events: starting a game, finishing a game, actions taken, duration played, final score, round reached, number of undos used.
- Per-card data: which cards appeared in your deck, how often each was stored, and which side of each card was active at end-of-round.
- App performance: errors and crashes that occur during play.

We use this to understand how the game is played, balance difficulty, and fix bugs.

### Device identifiers (automatic)
- An anonymous installation identifier generated by Unity's analytics SDK, tied to this app on this device. It resets if you reinstall the app.
- Basic device information: device model, operating system version, app version, language, country.

We do **not** collect your phone number, advertising ID, precise location, contacts, photos, microphone, or camera data.

### Optional playtester email (you choose)
- During playtesting, the Settings screen has an optional "Tester Email" field. If you enter an email there, it is attached to the gameplay events described above so we can tie feedback back to a specific tester.
- This field is **optional**. Leaving it blank means no email is ever sent.
- After public launch, this field will be removed from the Settings screen.

---

## 2. How the data is sent and stored

All data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections.

Data is received and stored by:
- **Unity Gaming Services (Analytics)** — Unity Technologies stores the gameplay events and device identifiers on servers operated by Unity. See the [Unity Services privacy policy](https://unity.com/legal/privacy-policy).
- **Portal Dragon LLC's private analytics sheet** — a copy of each completed game's summary is sent to a private Google Sheet owned by Portal Dragon LLC. Only authorized Portal Dragon personnel can read it.

We do not sell, rent, or trade any collected data to third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

---

## 3. Data saved on your device

The following is stored locally on your device and never leaves it:
- Your game preferences (volume, animation toggles, effects).
- The current game's autosave so you can resume mid-game.
- Your feat-unlock progress and high-score records.

You can clear this at any time by uninstalling the app or by using the "Reset Save Data" button in the in-game Settings screen.

---

## 4. Your rights

You can:
- **Request deletion** of the analytics data we hold tied to your installation or playtester email. Email **info@portaldragon.com** with the request.
- **Stop further analytics collection** by uninstalling the app, which invalidates the installation identifier.
- **Play offline**. The core game does not require an internet connection; analytics simply fail silently when offline.

For users in the European Union / UK: you have rights under the GDPR to access, correct, or erase personal data, and to object to processing. Contact us at **info@portaldragon.com** to exercise any of these rights.

For California residents: you have rights under the CCPA. We do not sell personal information as that term is defined in the CCPA. Contact us at the same address above.

---

## 5. Children

Palm Island is rated for general audiences but is not specifically designed for, or directed to, children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, contact us at **info@portaldragon.com** and we will delete it.

---

## 6. Security

Analytics data is transmitted over HTTPS. Data stored by Unity Gaming Services is protected by Unity's security practices. Data written to the Portal Dragon analytics sheet is protected by a shared-secret token and the sheet itself is access-controlled to Portal Dragon personnel only.

No system is perfect. If you believe there has been a breach of your data in connection with Palm Island, please notify us immediately at **info@portaldragon.com**.

---

## 7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time — for example, if we add new analytics, remove the playtester email field, or change how data is stored. When we do, we'll update the "Last updated" date above and, if the change is material, announce it in-app on next launch.
